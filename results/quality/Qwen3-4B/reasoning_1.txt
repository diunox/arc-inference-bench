We are given:

- **5 machines take 5 minutes to make 5 widgets.**

We are asked:

- **How long would it take 100 machines to make 100 widgets?**

---

### Step 1: Understand the rate of production

From the given information:

- 5 machines make **5 widgets in 5 minutes**.

This means:

- **1 machine makes 1 widget in 5 minutes**.

So, the **rate of production per machine is 1 widget per 5 minutes**.

---

### Step 2: Find the time for 100 machines to make 100 widgets

If **1 machine makes 1 widget in 5 minutes**, then:

- **100 machines can make 100 widgets in the same amount of time**, because each machine is making 1 widget in 5 minutes.

So, **100 machines can make 100 widgets in 5 minutes**.

---

### Final Answer:

**It would take 100 machines 5 minutes to make 100 widgets.**
