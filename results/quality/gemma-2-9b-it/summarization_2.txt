## Microservices vs. Monolithic Architecture:

| Feature | Microservices | Monolithic Architecture |
|---|---|---|
| **Scalability** | Highly scalable, individual services can be scaled independently | Limited scalability, entire application needs to be scaled |
| **Development** | Faster development cycles, teams can work on independent services | Slower development, changes require deploying the whole application |
| **Maintainability** | Easier to maintain and debug, isolated failures don't affect the whole system | Harder to maintain, changes can have unintended consequences across the entire application |
| **Complexity** | Higher complexity due to distributed systems and communication | Simpler architecture, easier to understand and manage initially |
