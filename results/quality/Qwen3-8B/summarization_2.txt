| **Aspect**                | **Microservices**                                 | **Monolithic Architecture**                        |
|--------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------|
| **Scalability**          | Easy to scale individual services as needed.    | Scaling requires scaling the entire application.  |
| **Deployment**          | Each service can be deployed independently.      | Entire application must be deployed together.     |
| **Development & Testing**| Faster development with smaller, focused teams.  | Complex testing due to tightly coupled components.|
| **Maintenance & Complexity** | More complex to manage due to distributed nature. | Simpler to maintain with centralized codebase.    |
