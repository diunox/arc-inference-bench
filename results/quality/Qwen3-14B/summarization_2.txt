Here's a comparison table summarizing the pros and cons of **microservices** versus **monolithic architecture** in four key areas:

| **Aspect**               | **Microservices**                                                                 | **Monolithic Architecture**                                                  |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Scalability**          | **Pro:** Easier to scale individual services as needed.                         | **Con:** Scaling the entire application is required, which can be inefficient. |
| **Deployment & Maintenance** | **Pro:** Independent deployment of services; easier to update and maintain.     | **Con:** Full redeployment is needed for any change, which can be time-consuming. |
| **Development Speed**    | **Con:** More complex to develop and integrate due to distributed nature.       | **Pro:** Simpler to develop and test as a single unit.                       |
| **Fault Isolation**      | **Pro:** Failures are isolated to individual services, minimizing system-wide downtime. | **Con:** A failure in one part of the system can bring down the entire application. |
